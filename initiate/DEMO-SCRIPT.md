# Peptide Portal Demo Script

*Demo Duration: 3-5 minutes*

---

## Opening (30 seconds)

**Host screen:** Open to `/provider` landing page

> "Hi everyone, I'm Kai with Peptide Portal. We help physicians in Utah scale their peptide therapy practices with automated intake, recurring revenue, and full compliance. Let me show you how it works."

---

## Step 1: Patient Experience (1 minute)

### 1.1 Landing Page
**Navigate to:** `/`
- Point out: Clean cream/gold design, professional feel
- "This is what your patients see when they first visit"

### 1.2 Take Quiz
**Click:** "Get started" → `/quiz`

> "Patients start with a free consultation — just a few questions about their health goals."

Walk through:
- Experience level selection
- Goals (weight loss, recovery, etc.)
- Age range
- Medical conditions
- Lifestyle (activity, sleep)
- Physical details (weight, height)

**Click:** "See My Protocol"

> "Based on their answers, we recommend a personalized peptide protocol with pricing."

### 1.3 Patient Dashboard
**Navigate to:** `/dashboard` (if logged in) or show screenshot

> "Once approved, patients get access to their dashboard where they can track their protocol, see shipment status, and message their provider."

---

## Step 2: Provider Experience (1.5 minutes)

### 2.1 Provider Landing
**Navigate to:** `/provider`

> "Now let's look at it from the provider's side."

Point out:
- Value propositions (automated intake, recurring revenue, HIPAA compliant)
- Pricing tiers ($199/mo for 25 patients, $399/mo for 100 patients)
- "Start Free Pilot" CTA

### 2.2 Provider Dashboard
**Navigate to:** `/provider/login` → sign in → `/provider/dashboard`

> "This is the provider dashboard. When a patient submits their intake, it shows up here for review."

Show:
- Intake queue with patient info
- Goals, experience level, contraindications
- "Approve" and "Deny" buttons

### 2.3 Approve Patient
**Click:** "Approve" on an intake

> "One click to approve — creates prescription, sets up order, notifies patient."

---

## Step 3: Key Features (1 minute)

### 3.1 Messages
**Navigate to:** `/dashboard/messages`

> "Secure messaging between patients and providers. HIPAA-compliant — no WhatsApp, no Signal."

### 3.2 Catalog
**Navigate to:** `/catalog`

> "13 peptide protocols seeded — Tirzepatide, BPC-157, Semax, and more."

### 3.3 Provider Page
**Navigate to:** `/provider` → scroll to pricing

> "Simple pricing. No per-patient fees. Cancel anytime."

---

## Closing (30 seconds)

**Return to:** `/provider`

> "That's Peptide Portal. Automate your patient intake, build recurring revenue, stay compliant. 
Start your 30-day free pilot at peptideportal.com/provider.
Any questions?"

---

## Demo Tips

✅ **Do:**
- Keep it under 5 minutes
- Show the approve button — it's the "wow" moment
- Mention Utah focus
- Have browser bookmarks ready

❌ **Don't:**
- Don't show signup flow (too long)
- Don't demo Stripe checkout (demo mode)
- Don't read every field on screens
